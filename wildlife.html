🌿 Wildlife & Nature

Discover Odisha’s and India’s breathtaking wildlife sanctuaries, bird havens, tiger reserves, and mangrove ecosystems.

🐅 Wildlife Sanctuaries

Simlipal Tiger Reserve

Home to Royal Bengal Tigers, elephants, and lush waterfalls.

📍 Mayurbhanj, Odisha

Chilika Bird Sanctuary

Asia’s largest brackish lagoon, home to flamingos and migratory birds.

📍 Chilika Lake, Odisha

Nandankanan Zoological Park

World-famous for white tigers and open safari experience.

📍 Bhubaneswar, Odisha

🌳 Tiger Reserves & Mangroves

Satkosia Tiger Reserve

Famous for its tiger habitat, Mahanadi gorge, and crocodile population.

📍 Angul District, Odisha

Bhitarkanika Mangroves

India’s second largest mangrove ecosystem, known for saltwater crocodiles.

📍 Kendrapara, Odisha

Sundarbans Mangroves

World’s largest mangrove forest, home to the iconic Royal Bengal Tiger.

📍 West Bengal
