🏛 Odisha Heritage

Explore the timeless treasures of Odisha — from temples to wildlife sanctuaries, rivers to traditions.

📜 Glorious History

Kalinga War & Ashoka

The famous Kalinga war (261 BCE) changed Emperor Ashoka, spreading Buddhism across Asia. Region: Dhauli Hills, Bhubaneswar

Udayagiri & Khandagiri Caves

Ancient Jain rock-cut caves from 2nd century BCE with carvings of daily life. Region: Bhubaneswar

⛩ Temples & Monuments

Konark Sun Temple

A UNESCO site, built as a giant stone chariot. Region: Konark, Puri

Lingaraj Temple

Magnificent 11th-century Shiva temple, center of Kalinga architecture. Region: Bhubaneswar

🛕 Buddhist Heritage of Odisha

Ratnagiri

Odisha is home to the famous Diamond Triangle – Ratnagiri– which reflect its glorious Buddhist past with monasteries, stupas, and sculptures. Ratnagiri

Lalitgiri

One of the oldest Buddhist sites in Odisha, famous for stupas and relic caskets linked to Lord Buddha. Lalitgiri

🌊 Rivers & Aquatic Life

Chilika Lake

Asia’s largest brackish lagoon, home to dolphins & migratory birds. Region: Puri, Khurda, Ganjam

Mahanadi River

The lifeline of Odisha, supporting irrigation, culture, and festivals. Region: Cuttack, Sambalpur
