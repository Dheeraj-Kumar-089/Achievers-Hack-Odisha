Odisha has a rich history dating back over 2000 years. The land of Kalinga witnessed the great Kalinga War which transformed Emperor Ashoka. The state is home to ancient temples like Konark Sun Temple and Jagannath Temple that reflect the architectural and cultural excellence of the region.

üíÉ Famous Dance Forms

Odissi

The classical dance form rooted in temple traditions of Puri & Bhubaneswar.

Gotipua

Performed by young boys dressed as women, precursor to Odissi dance. Famous in Raghurajpur.

Mayurbhanj Chhau

A martial art-based dance, known for its vigor and masks. Popular in Mayurbhanj district.

Odissi Classical Music

One of India‚Äôs classical music forms, Odissi blends ragas and talas. Rooted in Jagannath temple traditions, famous in **Puri & Bhubaneswar**.

Dalkhai Folk Songs

Energetic group songs sung by women during festivals like Nuakhai. Popular in **Sambalpur, Balangir, Kalahandi**.

Sambalpuri Folk

Famous for its rhythmic beats & lively lyrics, often with Dhol & Nishan. Thrives in **Western Odisha (Sambalpur, Bargarh)**.

The grand chariot festival of Lord Jagannath, celebrated in Puri with millions of devotees.
